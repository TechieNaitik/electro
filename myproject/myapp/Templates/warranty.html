Manufacturer & Store Warranty

We stand behind the quality of every tech product we sell. Most products on Electro come with both a Manufacturer Warranty and our own Store Guarantee.

Standard Hardware Warranty

Most electronics include a 1-year limited warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

Electro Plus Plan

Optional extended coverage is available for up to 3 years, including accidental damage protection for screens and water damage.

What is Covered?
  • Technical malfunctions (e.g., battery failure, screen flickering).
  • Hardware defects present at the time of purchase.
  • Internal circuit failures.
What is NOT Covered?
  • Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents) caused by regular use.
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications.
  • Loss or theft of the device.
Pro Tip: Register your device within 14 days of purchase to activate full manufacturer benefits.
