Hassle-Free Returns

At Electro, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your electronics, we're here to help.

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.

Original Packaging

Your item must be in the original packaging. This includes all manuals, cables, and accessories that were included in the box.

Proof of Purchase

Your item needs to have the receipt or proof of purchase (Order ID) from our store.

Refund Process

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment.
