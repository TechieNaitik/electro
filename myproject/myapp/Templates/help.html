How to Place an Order

Shopping at Electro is simple and secure. Follow these steps to place your first order:

  1. Browse our categories or use the search bar to find your desired electronics.
  2. Click on the product to view detailed specifications and features.
  3. Click "Add to Cart" to save the item. You can continue shopping or go to your cart.
  4. In the cart page, review your items and click "Proceed to Checkout".
  5. Enter your billing details and select your preferred payment method.
  6. Review your order and click "Place Order". Your electronics will be on their way!

Payment Methods

We offer various secure payment options for a smooth checkout experience:

  • UPI: Pay via Google Pay, PhonePe, or BHIM.
  • Cards: All major Credit and Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay).
  • Net Banking: Direct payment from all leading banks.
  • EMI: Available on selected credit cards and products.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for eligible pin codes and orders.

Account Management

Manage your Electro profile and preferences with ease:

  • Profile Settings: Update your name, email, and primary contact number from your dashboard.
  • Security: Change your password regularly to keep your account safe. Use the "Forgot Password" link if you cannot access your account.
  • Wishlist: Save products you love for later by clicking the heart icon on any product card.
  • Order History: View your past purchases, current order status, and download invoices directly from your account.
  • Address Book: Save multiple shipping addresses to make your checkout process even faster.
