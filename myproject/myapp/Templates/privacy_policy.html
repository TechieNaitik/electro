At Electro, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, billing details, and order IDs.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, and interaction patterns on our site.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and session data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Communicate with you about your account, orders, and promotions.
  • Improve our website, products, and services.
  • Send transactional emails such as OTPs and order confirmations.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit certain features of our website.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors, shipping partners) solely to fulfil your order. All third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure session management, and CSRF protection to safeguard your data. While no system is 100% immune to breaches, we are committed to keeping your information as secure as possible.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of our platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
